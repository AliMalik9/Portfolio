## Vector to 3D

Convert 2D text and vector layers into 3D models with one click, using a built-in ray tracing renderer. Create stunning 3D icons, banners, and mockups without needing to be a 3D specialist.

![Image 02](/images/blogs/top-9-figma-plugins-you-cant-miss-in-2025/img-02.jpg)

## AI Design Reviewer

Get instant feedback and suggestions to enhance web designs, improve copy, catch UI bugs, and ensure accessibility standards are met.

![Image 03](/images/blogs/top-9-figma-plugins-you-cant-miss-in-2025/img-03.jpg)

## Cassini Copilot

A collaborative toolkit for product teams that integrates into your browser or Figma workspace, making the product development process smoother and more organized.

![Image 04](/images/blogs/top-9-figma-plugins-you-cant-miss-in-2025/img-04.gif)

## Circular Text

Generate circular and arc text in your designs, with adjustable settings to create text that follows any angle.

![Image 05](/images/blogs/top-9-figma-plugins-you-cant-miss-in-2025/img-05.jpg)

## Noise & Texture

Create seamless tiled noise, textures, patterns, gradients, and more. Customize canvas size and default colors to fit your design needs.

![Image 06](/images/blogs/top-9-figma-plugins-you-cant-miss-in-2025/img-06.jpg)

## Instance Finder

Locate all instances of a component used in your file, organized by page for easy navigation.

![Image 07](/images/blogs/top-9-figma-plugins-you-cant-miss-in-2025/img-07.jpg)

## Animate on Path

Select an object and a curve for it to follow, creating dynamic animations with ease.

![Image 08](/images/blogs/top-9-figma-plugins-you-cant-miss-in-2025/img-08.jpg)

## Design System Organizer

Bulk swap instances and styles between master components, manage pathnames, and copy styles between different files.

![Image 09](/images/blogs/top-9-figma-plugins-you-cant-miss-in-2025/img-09.gif)

## Find and Replace Styles

Find and replace styles across components, instances, and library styles, ensuring pixel-perfect designs across projects.

![Image 10](/images/blogs/top-9-figma-plugins-you-cant-miss-in-2025/img-10.jpg)
